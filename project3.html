Lake Mckenzie, Australia

Lake Mckenzie is a beautiful lake. It has clear water and white sand. This lake is a great place for a picnic. You can also relax and go swimming. It is never crowded especially for a great big beautiful lake. You can go paddle boarding and kayaking. There is a carpark near by and changing rooms.The prefer no sunscreen when going in the lake because they don't want the water dirty. If you don't want sun there are trees for shade. You can also take a walk along the water or in the woods.
